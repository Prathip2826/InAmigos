Who We Are

Building a Better India, Together

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We are a Section 8 registered non-profit licensed by the Central Government.

Our mission is to create positive societal change through community empowerment and sustainable development — reaching children, women, animals, and underserved communities across multiple Indian states.

Flagship Projects

Six powerful programs creating real, measurable change across communities in India.

Project SEVA

Distributing food, clothing, and essential supplies to underprivileged individuals and families across India. Every meal served is a step toward dignity.

Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education, study materials, and mentorship for underprivileged children in rural areas, building bright futures one class at a time.

Project Udaan

Uplifting women through digital literacy, vocational training, financial independence, and leadership development. Udaan means flight — towards freedom and opportunity.
